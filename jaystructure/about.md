  <h1 class="post-title">Hi, I'm Jay.</h1>

  <div class="post-content">
    <p>
      I'm a Site Engineer based in Sydney, Australia, working on large-scale high-rise residential developments. I hold a Master of Engineering (Structural) from UTS and have spent the past five years on some of Sydney's most complex construction projects.
    </p>

    <h2>What I Write About</h2>
    <p>
      I started this site because I kept noticing the same gap: the standards and manuals that govern Australian construction are incredibly dense, and most engineers, builders, and students struggle to extract what's actually useful from them. I write to bridge that gap.
    </p>
    <p>
      I cover three main areas:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Construction & Engineering</strong> — NCC compliance, Australian Standards, structural principles, high-rise construction practice.</li>
      <li><strong>Bible & Scripture</strong> — Biblical history, ancient civilisations, archaeology, and deep dives into Scripture.</li>
      <li><strong>Architecture Marvels</strong> — The engineering stories behind the world's most extraordinary structures.</li>
    </ul>
